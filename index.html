Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE members enjoy reduced rates on many proprietary products and services including IEEE books and eBooks, journals and articles, conferences and proceedings, standards, society memberships, and continuing education courses.

Tech Srijan

TechSrijan, the annual technical fest of MMMUT, is an exhilarating celebration of innovation and technical excellence. Bringing together students from across the nation, the fest offers a dynamic platform to showcase skills in various fields such as robotics, coding, gaming, entrepreneurship, and debates. It fosters collaboration, creativity, and friendly competition through a series of challenging events and workshops. Whether you're a seasoned tech enthusiast or a beginner eager to learn, TechSrijan promises an engaging experience filled with opportunities for growth, learning, and fun. Join us for TechSrijan 2024, and be a part of the excitement!
